    Cardiology

    Comprehensive cardiac care from diagnosis to advanced interventional procedures.

    Our Cardiology department offers complete heart care including ECG, echo, angiography, angioplasty, pacemaker implantation, and open-heart surgery. Our cardiologists work round the clock to manage acute cardiac events and chronic heart conditions.

    Neurology

    Expert care for brain, spine, and nervous system disorders.

    The Neurology department at White Lotus Hospital manages stroke, epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and complex spinal conditions. We offer advanced neuro-imaging, EEG, EMG, and minimally invasive neurosurgery.

    Orthopedics & Joint Replacement

    Advanced musculoskeletal care for bones, joints, and sports injuries.

    From fracture management to complex joint replacement surgeries (hip, knee, shoulder), our orthopedic specialists use the latest implants and minimally invasive techniques for faster recovery and better outcomes.

    Pediatrics & Neonatology

    Dedicated healthcare for children from birth through adolescence.

    Our Pediatrics department provides comprehensive child healthcare including neonatology, immunization, growth monitoring, and management of childhood diseases. We have a dedicated NICU for critical newborn care.

    Oncology & Cancer Care

    Integrated cancer treatment combining surgery, chemotherapy, and radiation.

    White Lotus Hospital's Oncology Centre offers multidisciplinary cancer care including medical oncology, surgical oncology, radiation therapy, and bone marrow transplantation, supported by advanced diagnostics and palliative care.

    Gynecology & Obstetrics

    Comprehensive women's healthcare from adolescence to menopause.

    Our Gynecology department provides complete women's health services including prenatal care, normal and high-risk pregnancy management, laparoscopic surgeries, fertility treatments, and menopause care. We have a dedicated NICU for safe deliveries.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Common questions about our services, appointments, and facilities

    How do I book an appointment at White Lotus Hospital?

    You can book an appointment online through our website, call us at +91 22 4895 6000, or visit our OPD reception directly. Online appointments can be scheduled for in-person, tele, or phone consultations.

    What are the consultation fees?

    Initial consultation fee is ₹2,500. Follow-up consultations within 30 days are charged at ₹1,700. Fees may vary by specialist. Please check with the respective department for specific pricing.

    Does White Lotus Hospital accept insurance?

    Yes, we are empanelled with all major insurance providers including HDFC, ICICI, Star Health, and many more. Please carry your insurance card and pre-authorization documents at the time of your visit.

    What are the hospital visiting hours?

    OPD is open Monday to Saturday, 8AM to 10PM. Our Emergency Department operates 24×7. ICU visiting hours are from 10AM–11AM and 5PM–6PM. Please check with the ward for specific timings.

    Does the hospital have ambulance services?

    Yes, White Lotus Hospital operates a fleet of fully-equipped ambulances for emergency transfers and patient transport. Call our emergency line at +91 98200 11200 for immediate dispatch.

    Do you offer teleconsultation services?

    Yes, we offer teleconsultation and phone consultation services for follow-up patients and those unable to visit the hospital in person. Initial consultation: ₹2,500, Follow-up: ₹1,700 (within 30 days).
